About Anointed Flames

Anointed Flames was born from a vision to create a digital space where Christians from all walks of life can connect, grow, and encourage one another in faith.

We believe technology can be harnessed to strengthen spiritual connections and make discipleship accessible to everyone, everywhere.

Our Vision

To become the leading global platform for Christian fellowship, where every believer finds community, support, and spiritual nourishment.

Our Mission

To connect Christians worldwide through innovative technology that facilitates prayer, Bible study, and authentic relationships centered on Christ.
